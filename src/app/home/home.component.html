THE DISASTER BUTTON


Click the button for a true tale of horror history from Chicago, dating from the 1850s-1950s. Not for the faint of heart, some stories are funny, some are sad, some are bloody, and some are just bizarre.








All stories are sourced from Dead in Chicago , a horror history site maintained by software engineer and historian Leyla Royale from 2014 - 2017. Any editing errors can be blamed on past Leyla. All information is sourced from Chicago newspaper archives.
